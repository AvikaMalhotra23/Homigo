# Homigo 🏠✨
> **The Smart Roommate Matching & Student Housing Ecosystem**

Homigo is a high-fidelity mobile application designed to solve student accommodation challenges. By leveraging smart search, structured onboarding profiles, and lifestyle compatibility matching, Homigo connects university students with compatible roommates, manages shared expenses, coordinates events, and connects campus communities.

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive 11-Step Onboarding Wizard**:
  - Gathers academic profiles, school affiliations (e.g. LPU's departments), and lifestyle preferences.
  - Dynamically changes layout colors based on gender selection (soft sky blue for boys, baby pink for girls).
- **Premium Selection UI**:
  - Option cards scale up dynamically with smooth spring physics when selected.
  - Continuous shining shimmer overlays sweep selected items to mimic modern website highlights.
- **Smart Campus Hostel Grid**:
  - Automatically loads and displays university-specific hostels (e.g. boys BH-1 to BH-10, girls GH-1 to GH-9 for Lovely Professional University).
- **AI Compatibility Calculations**:
  - Displays progress indicators for calculated metrics (lifestyle, food preference, study, sleep schedules, cleanliness).
- **Ecosystem Features**:
  - Chat rooms, roommate requests, splitwise-style expense management, local services guide, reviews, and community marketplace feeds.

## 🏫 Why Every University Should Encourage Homigo

Universities face significant administrative overhead due to roommate mismatches, room change requests, and off-campus housing disputes. Homigo provides a solution that benefits the institution and the student body alike:

1. **Drastically Reduces Roommate Conflicts**:
   - Instead of random matching, Homigo matches students based on strict compatibility metrics (Veg/Non-Veg, Sleep/Wake cycles, Study schedules, Cleanliness ratings). Fewer conflicts mean fewer room-swap requests for hostel wardens.
2. **Promotes a Safe, Verified Community**:
   - Signups require university email authentication, roll numbers, sections, and schools. Only verified campus students can join.
3. **Fosters Campus Collaboration**:
   - Integrates local services maps, study groups, split expenses, and verified peer-to-peer campus marketplaces for used books, electronics, and mattress setups.
4. **Improves Academic Performance**:
   - Placing students in a study-aligned, stress-free housing environment has a direct, positive correlation with higher academic performance and overall student well-being.
